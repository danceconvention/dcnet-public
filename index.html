Welcome to danceConvention.net.

This is an online system for dance event management. It covers all possible aspects of dance event administration, providing a lot of intuitive yet powerful tools to manage registrations, payments, competitions and much more.

Features overview

Here's a very basic list of instruments we provide:

  • easy event configuration and management
  • online registration services
  • billing and accounting, including online payments processing
  • e-mail notification system
  • registration desk support
  • competitions management and scoring
  • reporting and publishing competition results
  • mobile clients for cash desk and contest check-in

Terms of use

We provide a variety of account plans to suit your needs. Basically, there are four types of accounts:

  • Demo: good for getting accustomed with the system. Free of charge, allows you to create and manage events up to 30 participants
  • Small: literally, for small events. Events are charged per participant.
  • Medium: for promoters running a few annual events, with fixed price for a single event.
  • Pro: annual subscription that allows to run an unlimited number of events without restrictions.

Support or Contact

Interested? Contact info@danceconvention.net and ask your questions. Also, feel free to register a demo promoter account at https://danceconvention.net/ and experiment with our system.
